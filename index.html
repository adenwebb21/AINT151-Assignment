The nightmare of Fandalin

This game is a fairly linear story experience - but you will have to search for the information you need!

You are playing as a wandering adventurer. You had heard there was something strange going on in the town of Fandalin and decided to investigate. You'll be meeting some of the residents of the town and trying to help them with what they need.

When you're ready to play, go to the menu in the top left hand corner and click play game! Good luck...
